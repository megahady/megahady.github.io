Nonlinear Control Systems

Welcome, aspiring control and robotics engineers, to an exciting journey into the world of robotics and automation! I'm Mohamed Abdelhady, your instructor and guide in this innovative and dynamic field.




Course Objectives:

  • Understand the fundamentals of nonlinear control systems theory.
  • Learn the analysis and design techniques for nonlinear control systems.
  • Explore stability concepts for nonlinear systems and equilibrium points.
  • Study time-varying and perturbed systems and their control strategies.
  • Examine passivity, memoryless functions, and their implications in control.
  • Understand input-output stability and L stability criteria.
  • Analyze feedback systems for stability and performance.
  • Explore special forms of nonlinear systems and their control implications.
  • Learn state feedback and robust stabilization techniques for nonlinear systems.
  • Study nonlinear observers and their role in system state estimation.
  • Examine output feedback techniques for stabilizing nonlinear systems.
  • Understand concepts of tracking, regulation, and performance optimization in nonlinear control.
  • Explore current trends and future directions in nonlinear control systems.
